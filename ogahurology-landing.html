About Us

Dedicated to Excellence in Urological Health

Ogah Urology Hospital was founded with a single mission: to bring world class urological care to Nigeria and beyond. Our team of fellowship trained specialists combines decades of clinical experience with the latest minimally invasive surgical techniques.

From routine consultations to complex reconstructive procedures, we offer comprehensive urological services in a state of the art facility designed around patient comfort, safety, and exceptional outcomes.

Fellowship trained, internationally recognised urologists

Cutting edge robotic and laparoscopic surgical suites

Patient first culture with personalised treatment plans

Questions & Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

We treat a comprehensive range of urological conditions including prostate disorders (BPH, cancer, prostatitis), kidney stones, bladder diseases, urinary incontinence, male infertility, erectile dysfunction, UTIs, and paediatric urological conditions. Our team handles both medical management and surgical intervention.

No referral is required. You can book a self-referral consultation directly through our website, phone line, or reception desk. However, if you have a referral letter from your GP or family physician, please bring it along as it helps provide valuable background information.

An initial consultation typically lasts 30–45 minutes. This includes a full history review, physical examination, and discussion of any preliminary tests or imaging. Follow up appointments are usually 15–30 minutes depending on the complexity of your case.

Yes. We are accredited by the National Health Insurance Scheme (NHIS) and partner with most major HMOs in Nigeria including Hygeia, AXA Mansard, Leadway Health, and more. Please contact our billing office to verify your specific plan coverage before your appointment.

Where clinically appropriate, we always favour minimally invasive approaches — laparoscopic, endoscopic, and robotic assisted techniques - to reduce surgical trauma, minimise scarring, shorten hospital stay, and speed up your recovery. Our surgeons are highly trained in these advanced methods.
