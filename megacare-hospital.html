✦ What We Do

Our Medical Services

Services that will leave you a Happy Satisfied Client - delivered by specialists committed to your wellbeing.

Internal Medicine

Specialist consultants in Cardiology, Endocrinology, Gastroenterology, Pulmonology, Nephrology and more.

Neo-Natal & Paediatric

State-of-the-art incubators, photo therapies, exchange blood transfusion and daily Paediatric Clinics.

Obstetrics & Gynaecology

Advanced delivery and feto-maternal care with experienced O&G consultants and trained midwives.

Neurosurgical Services

Specialist management of brain tumours, spinal cord injuries, neck pain, head injuries and more.

Eye Care

Comprehensive eye examinations, treatment of eye conditions, and corrective procedures by specialist ophthalmologists.

Orthopaedics

Treatment of bone fractures, joint replacements, sports injuries and musculoskeletal conditions.

Laboratory Services

Comprehensive diagnostic tests, blood work, imaging and pathology with rapid turnaround results.

Emergency Care

Round-the-clock emergency response with fully equipped trauma units and rapid specialist deployment.
