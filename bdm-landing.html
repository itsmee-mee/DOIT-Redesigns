Our Story

Born From Streets,
Built For Kings

BDM - Big Daddy Marshall, didn't start in a boardroom. It started with a vision: that every man and woman walking these streets deserves to look like royalty without compromising on comfort, quality, or attitude.

We craft garments that speak before you do. Each piece is a statement - deliberate, powerful, and unforgettable. From Lagos to London, BDM is the uniform of the bold.

Our designs fuse West African heritage with cutting-edge global streetwear culture, delivering pieces that exist at the intersection of identity and artistry.
