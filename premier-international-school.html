FAQs

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before applying. Still have questions? WhatsApp us directly.

What is the school fee structure?
Our fees vary by class level. We offer competitive rates with flexible payment options. Please contact our admissions office or WhatsApp us for the full current fee schedule for 2025/2026.
What age does my child need to be for admission?
We accept children from 3 months (Creche) through age 17 (SS3). Each class has a recommended age range - contact us to confirm placement for your child.
Where is the school located?
Premier International School is located in Abuja, FCT, Nigeria. We are easily accessible. Exact address in the contact section below.
What curriculum does the school follow?
We follow the Nigerian National Curriculum (NERDC) enriched with British educational standards. Our students sit for WAEC, NECO, and we also prepare students for Cambridge qualifications.
Do you provide school bus services?
Yes, we operate a school bus service covering major routes in Abuja, and surrounding areas. Bus fees are separate from tuition - contact us for routes and pricing.
How do I apply for my child?
You can apply by filling out our online application form (click "Apply Now"), visiting the school in person, or reaching out to us via WhatsApp or phone. Our admissions team will guide you through every step.
